About the Designer

Here at Perfectly Fine Design we strive for excellence. That's what our top designer, Patrick Farnworth does. From Multimedia to Illustrations, Logo Design, and beyond, Patrick doesn't disappoint. From a very young age, Patrick has astounded people with his ability to adapt to change, and working under pressure.

Enough Talking about myself in third person. I am 29, a full time freelancer, father, and husband. I have had experience working with companies such as Dynamite Enterprises under Ethan Harrison, the drummer for The Nixon Rodeo, under Greg Brunette owner of BSI Apparel, who I had the pleasure of rebranding their company from Brunette's Sportswear. I am currently freelancing under the head of UX Spokane and owner if Milkshake Interactive, Brent Schneider.
In my free time (when I have it) I enjoy walking around my property and getting personal photos of the wildlife and flora that is in the Cheney Spokane forests.
